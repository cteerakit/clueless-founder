I used to think underperforming pages needed better UI.
Most of the time they needed better language.

A strong landing page answers three questions immediately:

- Who is this for?
- What painful problem does it solve?
- Why is this better than doing nothing?

Before tweaking layouts, rewrite the hero, subhead, and first call-to-action.
If clarity improves, conversion usually follows.
