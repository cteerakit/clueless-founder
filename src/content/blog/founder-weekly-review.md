Every Friday, I run the same review:

- Wins: What worked and why?
- Friction: What slowed us down repeatedly?
- Focus: What one thing matters most next week?

The point is not perfect planning.
The point is noticing patterns before they become expensive.

Small teams move fast. Reviews help them move in the same direction too.
