Building in public helped me attract users, partners, and accountability.
It also became a distraction when I treated posting as progress.

My rule now:

- Share decisions and results
- Skip live commentary on unresolved noise
- Batch content creation twice a week

Public writing is leverage when it reflects real work.
It is a trap when it replaces real work.
